What we do ... read more

Kettlebell Sport or Girevoy Sport is the traditional Russian sport of kettlebell lifting. Best described as “endurance weightlifting". Kettlebell Sport evolved from athletic and military circles and slowly spread to the wider population.

How we do it ... read more

Our training consists of various types of kettlebell lifts mixed with bodyweight exercises and stretching, all under an hour.

Why we train ... read more

We're looking to make better our overall quality of life by improving our strength throughout the entire body.
Kettlebell Sport blends strength, endurance, balance and coordination. You can build amazing mental tenacity which carries over to other aspects of life.

Who we are ... read more

We are kettlebel enthusiasts who like to come together and push ourselves towards our limits. We have daily jobs and we do not draw profit from each other.
